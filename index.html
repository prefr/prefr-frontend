When not to use

Prefr is not suitable for binding decision making. Prefr is a tool supposed to support your decision making, not to make decisions for you. Everyone, who knows the participation link of your prefr ballot, can add as many votes as he or she likes. Anyone can change the votes of others or pretend to be someone else. Prefr neither tracks participants nor verifies their identities.

Although prefr is open source and all data neccessary to calculate the outcome of a ballot will be available to you, prefr provides no way to verify that this data has not been tampered with.

Privacy

We use cookies/local storage for some features (e.g. the 'recent prefr ballots' list). Yet none of this data will be transmitted to our servers. Your Browser knows, which prefr ballots you looked at, our servers do not. If you clear your browser data everything we stored in cookies or local storage will be gone.

However, we do dot provide any means to secure the data you submitted in a prefer ballot. (e.g. text of options, details or participants' names, etc.).This data is not encrypted on our servers and anyone who knows the link to yoour prefr ballot can access it. We can and everyone who gets hold of our database can. So please be careful about what you post.
